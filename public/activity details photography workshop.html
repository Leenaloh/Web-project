photography Workshop

Category: Art

Start Date & Time: April 15, 2025 - 8am

End Date & Time: April 15, 2025 - 12pm

Maximum Registrations: 15 - 20 Participants

Club: Photography & Design Club

Description:

The Photography Workshop is an interactive session designed to teach participants the fundamentals of photography, camera handling, and creative composition. Whether using a professional camera or a smartphone, this workshop helps participants develop their skills in capturing stunning photos, understanding lighting, and exploring different photography styles.

Objectives:

  • Teach the basics of photography, including composition, lighting, and camera settings.
  • Explore different photography styles such as portrait, landscape, street, and macro photography.
  • Provide hands-on practice with cameras and editing techniques.
  • Encourage creativity and storytelling through visual art

Activity Structure:

  1. Introduction to Photography:
    • Understanding different types of cameras and their settings.
    • Basics of composition: rule of thirds, leading lines, and framing.
    • The role of lighting and exposure in photography.
  2. Hands-on Camera Practice:
    • Practicing different shooting modes (manual, aperture priority, shutter priority).
    • Exploring angles, perspectives, and motion photography.
    • Capturing portraits, landscapes, and candid shots.
  3. Creative Photography Techniques:
    • Using natural and artificial light effectively.
    • Experimenting with black-and-white photography and creative filters.
    • Storytelling through photography: how to capture emotions and moments.
  4. Photo Editing & Post-Processing
    • Introduction to basic editing software (Lightroom, Photoshop, mobile apps).
    • Enhancing colors, contrast, and sharpness for better images.
    • Ethical considerations in photo editing and digital manipulation.
  5. Photo Showcase & Feedback:
    • Participants present their best shots.
    • Constructive feedback to improve photography skills.
    • Tips on building a photography portfolio and social media presence.

Who Can Join?

  • Beginners and photography enthusiasts looking to improve their skills.
  • Anyone interested in capturing creative and meaningful photographs.
  • Future photographers, content creators, and visual storytellers.

This workshop helps participants develop their technical and artistic photography skills, allowing them to capture and share impactful visuals.
