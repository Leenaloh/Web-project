Robotics & AI Lab

Category: Technology

Start Date & Time: April 20, 2025 - 1pm

End Date & Time: April 20, 2025 - 6pm

Maximum Registrations: 10 - 15 Participants

Club: Robotics & AI Club

Description:

The Robotics and AI Lab is a hands-on session where participants explore the fundamentals of robotics and artificial intelligence, gaining practical experience in building, programming, and understanding intelligent systems. The lab encourages creativity, problem-solving, and innovation, making it ideal for students interested in robotics, automation, and AI technologies.

Objectives:

  • Introduce the basics of robotics, AI, and their real-world applications.
  • Provide hands-on experience in building and programming robots.
  • Explore AI concepts such as machine learning, computer vision, and automation.
  • Foster teamwork and innovation through interactive projects.

Activity Structure:

  1. Introduction to Robotics and AI:
    • What is robotics? Overview of robotic systems and components.
    • Understanding AI: Machine learning, neural networks, and automation.
    • Real-world applications: Robotics in healthcare, industry, and daily life.
  2. Hands-on Robotics Session:
    • Basics of robotics hardware (motors, sensors, microcontrollers like Arduino & Raspberry Pi).
    • Programming simple robot movements and functions.
    • Building a small robot or controlling a robotic arm.
  3. Exploring AI and Machine Learning:
    • Introduction to AI and how machines learn from data.
    • Hands-on AI demo: Creating a basic chatbot or image recognition model.
    • Ethical considerations and the future of AI.
  4. Project-Based Learning:
    • Group challenge: Building a robot that completes a specific task.
    • AI application project, such as facial recognition or automated decision-making.
    • Presentation and discussion of projects.
  5. Discussion & Career Insights:
    • How to start a career in robotics and AI.
    • Resources for learning (online courses, competitions, open-source projects).
    • Q&A session with experts or mentors.

Who Can Join?

  • Students interested in robotics, AI, and emerging technologies.
  • Beginners and advanced learners looking for hands-on experience.
  • Future engineers, developers, and tech innovators.

This lab provides an interactive environment for learning, creativity, and teamwork, helping participants build essential skills for the future of technology.
